# VERDICT DFIR SVG Usage Guide

## Source of truth
The individual SVG files in `svg_assets/` are production assets. `verdict-brand-board-reconstructed.svg` and its PNG are assembled from those assets as a visual QA proof.

## Logo rules
- Use the primary or stacked logo on Paper Cream or white.
- Use `verdict-icon-mono-dark.svg` on dark surfaces and `verdict-icon-mono-light.svg` on light surfaces.
- The app icon and social avatar contain their own dark field.
- Do not recolor the evidence branches independently from cobalt/lilac except in monochrome variants.
- Do not add glow, glitch, bevel, shadow, or rainbow effects.

## Semantic colors
- Seafoam = verified / match / passed.
- Signal Coral = rejected / mismatch / contradiction.
- Butter Yellow = review / note / attention.
- Cobalt and Lilac = brand, navigation, non-semantic highlights.

## Portability
All production SVG text has been converted to vector paths. No font files are included or required.
