VERDICT is free and open source (Apache-2.0). Clone it, self-host it, run it air-gapped — forever. The
paid offering is **help, not the software.**

## What's on offer

- **On-prem deployment** — stand up the toolchain (SIFT / Volatility 3 / the two MCP servers / the
  reference agent) in your SOC, hardened and reproducible. (Sells away the setup pain — the SIFT/
  Volatility/Rust-build work — the way a managed installer would.)
- **DFIR-for-agents training** — wiring VERDICT into your agents (Claude, ChatGPT, OpenCode, Cursor,
  Cline, …) and your incident-response workflow.
- **Support / SLA** — priority help for teams running it in production; upgrade guidance; custom typed
  tools built behind the allow-listed surface (never an `execute_shell`).

## What this will NOT do

- **Take custody of your evidence.** Everything runs on **your** infrastructure (or your own cloud
  tenancy). Your forensic data never leaves your control — that is the point.
- **Overclaim.** The product's honesty guardrails (`coverage_manifest.json`, the scoped verdict words,
  the maturity note) apply to the engagement too: an honest `INDETERMINATE` beats a confident wrong
  answer, in a paid engagement just as in the tool.
